Get Started with Simplifyd Biz Escrow

Follow these simple steps to secure your online transactions:

  2. Set Terms: Agree with the other party on the transaction terms.
  3. Deposit Funds: The buyer securely deposits funds into escrow.
  4. Complete Transaction: Once confirmed, funds are released to the seller.
